Myriam Schönenberger

I'm a master student at Cognitive Learning for Vision and Robotics Lab (CLVR) at KAIST advised by Prof. Joseph J. Lim.

During my physicist years at theInstitute for particle physics and astrophysics (IPA) at ETHZ where I was advised by Rainer Wallny, I searched for supersymmetry in the fully hadronic and Higgs to diphoton final states, which resulted in my PhD thesis.
